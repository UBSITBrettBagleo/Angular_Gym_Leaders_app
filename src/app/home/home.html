Pokémon Gym Leaders Database

Explore the Gym Leaders of the Kanto and Johto Regions.

Kanto Region

Kanto is the first region introduced in the Pokémon series. As well as the home of protagonist like Ash Ketchum, Red and Leaf. It is the home to legendary Gym Leaders such as Brock, Misty, and Giovanni. Trainers travel across eight cities to earn badges and challenge the Pokémon League.

Johto Region

Johto is located west of Kanto and was introduced in Pokémon Gold and Silver. The region rich on tradition, history, and legendary Pokémon.Johto introduces new Gym Leaders and explores the mysteries of legendary Pokémon.
