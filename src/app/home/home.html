Pokémon Gym Leaders Database

Explore the many Regions of the pokemon world.

Kanto Region

The original region featuring the first 8 Gym Leaders introduced in the Pokémon games. Trainers travel across eight cities to earn badges, battle evil and challenge the Pokémon League.

Johto Region

Region west of Kanto introduced in Pokémon Gold and Silver. The region rich on tradition, history, new legendary Pokémon, old foes, and new gym leaders to challenge.

Hoenn Region

A tropical region introduced in Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire. With diverse ecosystems, two new evil teams, never seen before Pokémon and new Gym leaders. Each with their own unique gimicks.
